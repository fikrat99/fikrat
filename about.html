We Are fikrat

Welcome to our Tourism Consultancy Company, based in the heart of Turkey! We are a team of dedicated professionals with expertise in Health, Tourism, Trade, Rental, and IT. Our company is committed to providing exceptional services to our clients and ensuring that their time in Turkey is both enjoyable and unforgettable.

Health Services:

Our company offers a comprehensive range of medical services to clients seeking healthcare in Turkey. We specialize in dental treatments, plastic surgery, and medical check-ups. Our team works closely with clients to create a personalized medical plan that caters to their specific needs.

Rental Services:

Our company offers rental services to individuals and businesses in Turkey. We provide affordable and convenient car rentals, boat rentals, and vacation rentals to ensure that our clients have a hassle-free experience while exploring Turkey.

We understand the importance of technology in today's business landscape. Our team of IT professionals provides a range of services, including website design, software development, and digital marketing. We are committed to helping businesses of all sizes succeed in the digital age.

In addition to our core services, we also offer a range of other services to make our clients' time in Turkey as enjoyable and stress-free as possible.
